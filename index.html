Schüler Treffen Flüchtlinge e.V. („Students meet refugees“) is a registered non-profit organization founded and guided by students from Berlin. Its purpose is to give young people from a migrant background the possibility for cultural exchange with German students and people. We host joint events for students from Germany and abroad to encourage communication and education on the different cultures. Such events are cooking together or exploring the city of Berlin. Our goal is to motivate students to take a broader part in social engagement in especially but not limited to intercultural exchange.

I am a full member and voluntary head web developer of STF Berlin e.V. since 2018, because in my opinion, everybody can and everybody should participate in making the world a better place.

Click to go to www.stfberlin.de

Aktion Zukunft 2018 (“Operation Future 2018”) is a multi-day workshop in Berlin organized by STF Berlin e.V. for students from migrant backgrounds in Germany. In our annual seminar we teach those students the techniques they require to develop and implement their own ideas and social projects in Germany.

Besides the main website of STF Berlin e.V., I am also constantly developing this website to fully represent our organization’s most successful venture.
