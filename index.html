One Future e.V. is a registered non-profit organization founded by students from Berlin. We host joint events for students from Germany and abroad with the goal to enable intercultural exchange and encourage students to take a broader part in social engagement.

I am the head web developer of One Future e.V. since 2018, because in my opinion, everybody can participate in making the world a better place.

Click to go to www.stfberlin.de

Aktion Zukunft 2018 (“Operation Future 2018”) is a multi-day workshop in Berlin organized by One Future e.V. for students from migrant backgrounds in Germany. In our seminar we teach those students the techniques they require to develop and implement their own ideas and social projects in Germany.

Besides the main website of One Future e.V., I am also constantly developing this website to fully represent our organization’s most successful venture.
